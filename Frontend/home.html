Our Vision

A world where incidents resolve themselves

We envision a future where engineering teams are never caught off-guard by production failures. IRA aims to build the world's most intelligent incident response layer — one that understands your infrastructure, learns from every incident, and acts before humans even notice a problem.

Our goal is to eliminate alert fatigue, reduce MTTR to near-zero, and let engineers focus on innovation instead of firefighting.

Our Mission

AI-driven reliability for every engineering team

Our mission is to democratize enterprise-grade incident response. We combine large language models with real-time observability data to automatically triage incidents, identify root causes, and suggest or execute remediation steps — all within seconds.

We are committed to building open, explainable AI tooling that engineering teams of all sizes can trust, integrate, and rely on during their most critical moments.

What IRA Does

Built for the modern DevOps team

From detection to remediation — AI at every step of your incident lifecycle.

Real-Time Alerting

Instant notifications for critical incidents with severity-based prioritisation and smart deduplication.

AI Agent Analysis

Claude-powered AI analyzes every incident, identifies root causes, and recommends precise remediation steps.

Analytics Dashboard

Full visibility into incident trends, resolution times, team performance, and severity breakdowns.

Auto Remediation

Step-by-step fix playbooks generated on the fly, tailored to your infrastructure and incident category.

Security Monitoring

Detect threats, brute-force attacks, and anomalous patterns with automated escalation workflows.

Incident Reports

One-click executive summaries and post-mortem reports with timeline, impact, and lessons learned.
